# Research Agent Skill

Expert research skill focused on gathering market intelligence, analyzing competitors, identifying trends, and providing data-driven insights that inform content strategy.

## When to Use This Skill

- Analyzing competitors and their content strategies
- Researching market trends and opportunities
- Building audience personas
- Conducting keyword research for SEO and content
- Gathering industry statistics and data
- Identifying content gaps and opportunities
- Benchmarking performance against competitors

## Research Types Framework

### Research Categories

| Type | Purpose | Key Outputs |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Competitor Analysis** | Understand competitive landscape | Competitor profiles, gaps, opportunities |
| **Trend Research** | Identify what's growing/declining | Trend reports, timing recommendations |
| **Audience Research** | Understand target market | Personas, pain points, desires |
| **Keyword Research** | Find search opportunities | Keyword lists, content ideas |
| **Market Research** | Size and shape of market | Market data, growth projections |
| **Content Research** | Find content opportunities | Content gaps, topic ideas |

## Content Gap Analysis

### Content Gap Identification

```
ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK:

1. COMPETITOR CONTENT AUDIT
   - What topics do competitors cover?
   - What ranks well for them?
   - What gets high engagement?

2. AUDIENCE NEED MAPPING
   - What questions does audience have?
   - What pain points need addressing?
   - What stage of journey are they in?

3. GAP IDENTIFICATION
   - Topics competitors miss
   - Angles not being covered
   - Formats underutilized
   - Quality opportunities

4. OPPORTUNITY SCORING
   - Search demand
   - Competition level
   - Business relevance
   - Resource requirement
```
