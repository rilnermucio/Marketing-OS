# Social Agent Skill

Expert social media skill focused on creating platform-optimized content that drives engagement, reach, and growth.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating posts for any social media platform
- Optimizing content for specific algorithms
- Planning posting schedules and frequency
- Writing platform-appropriate copy

## Platform Specifications

### Instagram

| Format | Dimensions | Specs |
|--------|------------|-------|
| Feed (Square) | 1080x1080 | 1:1 ratio, max 30MB |
| Feed (Portrait) | 1080x1350 | 4:5 ratio, best engagement |

## Algorithm Fundamentals

### The Universal Algorithm Equation

```
Visibility = f(Engagement, Relevance, Recency, Relationship)
```

| Factor | Description | How to Optimize |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Engagement | Interactions on content | Create shareable, saveable content |
| Relevance | Match to user interests | Use relevant keywords, hashtags |
| Recency | How new the content is | Post when audience is active |
| Relationship | Connection with viewer | Engage with audience, build community |

## Hooks by Platform

### Instagram Hooks (First Line)

```
Curiosity: "This changed everything about how I [topic]..."
Controversy: "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]"
Promise: "Here's exactly how I [achieved result]..."
Question: "Why does nobody talk about [topic]?"
Story: "6 months ago, I was [pain point]..."
List: "3 things I wish I knew about [topic]"
```

### LinkedIn Hooks (First Line)

```
Contrarian: "Everyone says [common belief]. I disagree."
Story: "I was rejected 47 times before [success]."
Data: "[Surprising statistic] ‚Äî here's why it matters."
Observation: "I've reviewed 500+ [thing]. Here's what I learned."
Challenge: "Stop doing [common mistake]. Do this instead."
Personal: "The hardest conversation I had this year..."
```

### TikTok Hooks (First 3 Seconds)

```
Direct: "If you [audience], you need to know this..."
Shock: "Nobody's talking about this and it's a problem..."
Tutorial: "Here's the [topic] hack everyone's asking about..."
POV: "POV: You finally discover [solution]..."
Trend: [Use trending sound/format + niche twist]
```

## Hashtag Strategy

### Instagram Hashtag Mix

| Category | Volume | Quantity | Purpose |
|----------|--------|----------|---------|
| High | 1M+ posts | 2-3 | Broad reach |
| Medium | 100K-1M | 3-4 | Balanced |
| Low | 10K-100K | 3-4 | Niche, targeted |
| Branded | Any | 1-2 | Recognition |

**Total: 10-15 hashtags** (place in caption or first comment)

### LinkedIn Hashtags

- Use 3-5 hashtags maximum
- Mix broad (#marketing) with specific (#contentmarketing)
- Check follower counts (aim for 10K-500K followers)
- Place at end of post or integrated naturally

### TikTok Hashtags

- Use 3-5 hashtags
- Always include at least one trending hashtag
- Mix #fyp or #foryou with niche tags
- Place in caption, not comments

## Engagement Tactics

### Comments Strategy

**On your posts:**
- Reply to every comment in first hour
- Ask follow-up questions
- Pin best comments
- Use comments as content ideas

**On others' posts:**
- Leave thoughtful, valuable comments
- Be early on posts from larger accounts
- Add insights, not just agreement
- Avoid generic comments ("Great post!")

### Story Engagement (Instagram)

- Use polls, questions, quizzes daily
- Respond to every DM and reaction
- Share user-generated content
- Use countdown for launches
- Add link stickers for CTAs
