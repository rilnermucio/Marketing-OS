# Clone Strategy

> See [CONNECTORS.md](../CONNECTORS.md) for connected services that can provide competitor data, screenshots, and analytics.

Analyze a competitor, creator, or brand's content strategy and reverse-engineer it into a complete, adapted plan for your own brand — maintaining your unique voice and differentiation.

## Trigger

This command is invoked when the user says `/clonar-estrategia` followed by a profile, brand, or URL, or when they ask to reverse-engineer, clone, or copy a competitor's strategy.

## Inputs

Gather the following information. If any required field is missing, ask the user before proceeding:

1. **Target Profile/Brand** (required) — Social media handle, website URL, or brand name to analyze
2. **Platforms to Analyze** (required) — Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube, website/blog
3. **Your Brand Context** (required) — Your niche, audience, voice, and what makes you different
4. **Analysis Depth** (optional) — Quick overview, standard analysis, deep dive
5. **Goal** (optional) — What you want to achieve: grow followers, increase engagement, drive sales, build authority
6. **Time Period** (optional) — Analyze last 30 days, 90 days, or all-time patterns

## Research Phase

### Phase 1: Deep Research

Use available tools (WebSearch, Playwright screenshots, MCP connectors) to gather:

**Content Audit:**
| Data Point | What to Find |
|------------|-------------|
| Posting frequency | Posts per week/day by platform |
| Content types | Carousel, reels, text, video, stories ratio |
| Top performing content | Highest engagement posts (last 30-90 days) |
| Content pillars | 3-5 recurring themes/topics |
| Formats used | Templates, structures, recurring formats |
| Hashtag strategy | Recurring hashtags, volume, categories |
| Visual identity | Colors, fonts, photography style, branding |
| Caption patterns | Length, structure, hooks, CTAs used |

**Engagement Analysis:**
| Data Point | What to Find |
|------------|-------------|
| Engagement rate | Avg likes/comments/shares per post |
| Comment patterns | What triggers most comments |
| Share triggers | What makes people share |
| Save triggers | What makes people save |
| Response strategy | How they respond to comments |
| Community building | Groups, challenges, user-generated content |

**Growth Signals:**
| Data Point | What to Find |
|------------|-------------|
| Follower growth | Estimated growth trajectory |
| Viral content | What went viral and why |
| Collaborations | Who they collaborate with |
| Cross-promotion | How they drive between platforms |
| Lead generation | How they capture emails/leads |
| Monetization | How they sell (products, services, sponsorships) |

### Phase 2: Strategy Extraction

From the research, extract:

**Content Formula:**
```
HOOK PATTERNS:
1. [Most used hook type + example]
2. [Second most used + example]
3. [Third most used + example]

CONTENT STRUCTURE:
[How they typically structure their content]

CTA PATTERNS:
1. [Primary CTA pattern]
2. [Secondary CTA pattern]

POSTING CALENDAR:
[Day-by-day posting schedule with content types]
```

**Growth Mechanics:**
```
PRIMARY GROWTH LEVER: [What drives most growth]
SECONDARY LEVER: [Supporting growth strategy]
ENGAGEMENT LOOP: [How they maintain engagement]
MONETIZATION PATH: [How content → revenue]
```

### Phase 3: Adaptation

Transform the strategy for your brand:

**Adaptation rules:**
1. **Keep the structure, change the substance** — Use their frameworks but with your expertise
2. **Maintain your voice** — Apply their posting cadence but your unique tone
3. **Differentiate on angles** — Cover similar topics from a different perspective
4. **Add your unfair advantage** — What can you do that they cannot?
5. **Respect IP** — Adapt patterns, never copy content directly
